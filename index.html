About

My name is Nick Castillo, and I am an engineer with a passion for blending hands-on technical work and programming to create practical solutions. I am a self-taught programmer with experience in Expo, React Native, HTML, and C++, and my main interests include robotics and artificial intelligence. My long-term aim is to apply these skills toward advancing renewable energy and sustainability.

I bring a unique blend of hands-on technical skills and programming expertise. With over eight years of experience in electrical, plumbing, and handyman work, I’ve developed a knack for solving problems quickly and effectively—whether that means crafting an immediate fix or developing a long-term solution. I believe practical problem-solving is essential, since overthinking can often delay progress and lead to significant costs in real-world projects.

In addition to programming, I am skilled in 3D modeling with Fusion 360 and have designed and prototyped my own creations. I enjoy identifying challenges, improving systems, and finding innovative ways to make things work better.

My goal is to continue growing as an engineer by securing an internship or joining a startup, where I can contribute my technical knowledge, hands-on experience, and problem-solving mindset to impactful projects.
